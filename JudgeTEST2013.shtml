Please read the following Notes and Instructions carefully before beginning the 2013/2014 Judges test.

Note: All HRC Judges are required to take a Rulebook Test every two (2) years in order to maintain their Judges License status. The purpose of the Judges test is to require those involved in the Judging of HRC Tests to periodically read the entire rulebook and become familiar with new Running Rules changes. Test must be taken by May 1, 2013, which gived you 5 months to take it.

Instructions: This is an open-book test based on the 2013/2014 HRC Rulebook. You may consult with your Field Representative or another Judge if you are unsure of any question. However, the completion of the test is to be by you and is based on your honor.

RESULTS WILL BE IMMEDIATELY GIVEN AFTER YOU SUBMIT BY TELLING YOU THAT YOU PASSED OR FAILED. KEEP THAT PAGE FOR YOUR RECORDS. THIS IS AN AUTOMATED TEST AND RESULTS ARE SENT TO HRC WITH THE OUTCOME OF THE TEST.
